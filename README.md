### MultiDict

MultiDict is a website for keeping track of words from various dictionaries and other sources. I can add entries consisting of words, their definitions, and their sources (if available). This format also allows me to add technical jargon from areas such as Mathematics, Computer Science, and Economics.

Most English entries are from [Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/), most Chinese entries are from [漢典](https://www.zdic.net/), and most other entries have links to [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Main_Page), which usually contains information that is accurate though somewhat abstruse.
